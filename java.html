Adventure Project

Description: For the Adventure project, we had to make a choose-your-own-adventure style of game that would give the player options and allow them to guide the story by picking one or the other. The player would type in the word that corresponded with the choice they wanted to make, and the game would continue based on what they typed. It was required to have at least four choices down any given path, however mine had one premature ending (The result of the player just making really bad decisions) and some options that allowed the player top make up to five decions before they got to the end.
